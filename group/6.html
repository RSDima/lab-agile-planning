The CAIR group at UKZN conducts research in machine learning in general and deep learning in particular. The group focuses on deep learning neural network and Bayesian deep learning approaches to solving problems in recommender systems, natural language processing, self driving cars and image processing and computer vision. The group investigates:

  • Deep Reinforcement Learning
  • Bayesian Deep Learning
  • Deep Neural Networks
